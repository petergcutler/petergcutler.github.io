From 2011-2015, I worked for FleishmanHillard creative teams. I worked across brand strategy, design, and UX, ultimately leading VOX Global's video & motion design group.

Sustainability for AT&T

Our firm produced AT&T's sustainability media & reporting. We built a range of web presences, supporting mitigation efforts including climate and carbon impact, and unique interventions like AT&T's campaign against texting while driving. Our media campaigns drove millions of impressions to their sites through advertising across both traditional paid media and social media. Our custom creative across web and video won AT&T CSR Webby Awards for over 5 years running.

Reducing Texting While Driving with AT&T & Werner Herzog

At FleishmanHillard, we grew AT&T's sustainability campaign against texting while driving, called "It Can Wait," to record success. This was an initiative that came out of our offices, and as it reached its heights, we collaborated with our sibling company, BBDO New York, and writer/director Werner Herzog, to produce a branded documentary around it. The film, "From One Second to the Next," was tweeted over 150,000 times and shown in over 40,000 high schools after it was produced, and it helped AT&T Sustainability have its biggest year in media to date.

Robert Wood Johnson Foundation & Easter Seals Online

We developed and managed online communications for many philanthropic organizations and non-profits. I supported Robert Wood Johnson Foundation and Easterseals both for a number of years. When RWJF contributed funding to the Center for Health and Health Care in Schools, we developed websites and content for them, where I contributed content audits, web design, and heuristic review. With Easterseals, we worked with their North Carolina branch contributing web and motion design.

Video Campaigns for Lumina Foundation

I developed motion graphics for many of our clients, including AT&T for their sustainability reporting, and Lumina Foundation for their efforts to increase educational attainment rates in the United States. I communicated with clients using early storyboards and animatics to nail down our visual and narrative approach, then developed final animations using a mix of Adobe After Effects and Maxon Cinema 4d. Each of these campaigns went on to win Hermes Awards.
